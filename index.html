About Us

Welcome to Cinnamon Hills, where tranquility meets luxury. Nestled amidst breathtaking cinnamon hills and lush paddy fields, our villa offers a serene escape for those seeking relaxation and adventure. Wake up to the soothing sounds of nature, explore the vibrant landscapes, and immerse yourself in the local culture. Located just minutes away from the bustling beaches of Hikkaduwa and conveniently positioned near the highway exit, Cinnamon Hills is your gateway to unforgettable experiences in Sri Lanka.
